Discover another world: learn to code!

Mobile application, social network, e-commerce site ... everyone is consuming code, but very few people understand it or know how to produce it: learn to code is to take the to be able to and become actor of the revolution digital.

Bonjour Monde is a 3 month program which allowslearn to code and to better understand our digital world.

It is a training in blended learning :  4 hours face-to-face per week supplemented by a learning in line. Everyone learns tohis rhythm and according to its needs : face-to-face training makes it possible to tackle conceptsof the week; online modules give the possibilityto deepenthe subject. Achat room is reserved for promotion and a former is available every day to answer questions.

This program does not require technical prerequisites individuals and focuses on the following values:  creativity, community, share, innovation, entrepreneurship and diversity.

Bonjour Monde is a real program ofinitiation to code who gives you the keys to address this digital world and create your project.
